Tobe and The Juice

Tobe Nwigwe is a Nigerian-American rapper, singer, and songwriter hailing from Houston, Texas. A menber of The Juice, he is known for his distinct style, he blends sharp lyricism with elements of hip-hop, soul, and Afrobeat. Tobe often collaborates with his wife, Fat, and producer, Nell, creating a dynamic trio. His rise to fame came through viral videos, showcasing his captivating performances and thought-provoking lyrics. With a strong independent presence, Tobe garnered widespread attention before signing with a major label, proving his ability to reach new heights while staying true to his cultural roots and unique artistry.

From there, The Juice has soared, gaining international recognition and landing in the top 10 on various music charts. New fans quickly gravitated to the group’s fresh and authentic sound, cementing their status as one of the most exciting and rapidly rising acts of the decade. Fans, who lovingly call themselves "The Blend," are captivated by their raw, high-energy performances. Tobe Nwigwe and The Juice are currently working on their next studio album, anticipated for release in early 2024, promising to continue their powerful momentum.
